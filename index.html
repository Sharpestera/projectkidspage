FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What age groups do Icon School Programs target?
Icon School programs target children and teenagers aged 6 to 16, with learning paths specifically designed to suit each age group and skill level.
Does my child need any prior programming experience to start with you?
No, your child doesn't need prior experience. Our programs are designed for beginners and start from the basics!
What is the difference between the Programming and Robotics tracks?
The Programming track focuses on teaching coding skills and creating software, while the Robotics track combines programming with building and controlling robots.
How can i track my child's progress throughout the course?
You can track your child's progress through regular updates from instructors, progress reports, and by reviewing completed projects and assignments.
